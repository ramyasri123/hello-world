BIGGEST AND SMALLEST

LARGEST TOOTHPICK

 DEC 3, 2015  

Dr. Livingstone Yesudason (born on May 12, 1955) of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, created a 10ft long tooth pick using Dill wood, on February 9, 2015 at Avadi,Chennai.

SMALLEST WOODEN COT

 NOV 4, 2015  

Parwati Jangid (Born on 24th May, 1993 ) Artist of Jodhpur, Rajasthan created a Smallest Wooden Cot, measuring 12 mm in Length and 4mm in Height using wooden and bamboo material on October 2, 2015 and it was displayed at Jodhpur, Rajasthan.

SMALLEST FLOUR MAKER

 NOV 4, 2015 

Parwati Jangid (Born on 24th May, 1993 ) Artist of Jodhpur, Rajasthan created a Smallest Flour Maker, measuring 8mm Lower circumference and 4.5mm in upper circumference using wooden and bamboo material on October 2, 2015 and it was displayed at Jodhpur, Rajasthan.

‘World’s Biggest Replica Postcard Addressed to Gandhiji’

 OCT 6, 2015 

M.M. Jasdanwala, proprietor of Kafka’s English Corner had organised  a project of ‘World’sbiggest replica postcard addressed to Gandhiji‘ in which 130 people had participated on 1st and 2nd October, 2015. The postcard was permanently exhibited at Rastrashala, Rajkot for public awareness.The postcard size was 8X4 single wooden sheet weighing approximately 40 kilograms and covered with sun-mica.

Biggest Laddu (Traditional Indian Sweet)

 OCT 1, 2015 

On the occasion of Vinayaka Chavithi 2015 (Festival of Lord Ganesha), Saladi Venkateshwara Rao S/o Satya Narayana Murthy from Sri Bhaktanjaneya Sweets House with 16 Members preparing a Biggest Laddu (Indian Traditional Sweet) weighing 8500 Kgs with in a 12 hours period using around 2,900 kg sugar, 2,300 kg Tur Dal, 1,100 kg ghee, 700 kg Sugar Chips, 800 kg Dry Fruits, 40 kg of Cardamom and 10 kg Camphor and it was displayed on September 17, 2015 at Tapeshwaram Village, Mandapeta Mandal, East Godawari Dist., Andhrapradesh, India.

LARGEST JAAPI

 MAR 2, 2015  

Subhash Medhi (Born on 31st December, 1985) of Morigaon , Assam,  has designed Largest Jaapi (Assamese Traditional headgear) 1,400 bamboos and 3,000 tokou (palm) leaves  measuring 545.965 sq.meter total surface area, including (80 Feet Diameter, Radius 40 Feet & Circumference 260 Feet). Total weight of Jappi was 10326.51 kg and it was displayed on 25th October, 2014 at  Bhurbandha Playground, District : Morigaon , Assam
