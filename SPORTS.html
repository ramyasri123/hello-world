Fastest Shooting by Sport Rifle

 JUL 25, 2014 
Divyarajsinh Bahadursinh Rana (Born on 8th November 1982) Sports Shooting Coach and Classification Officer of Porbandar, Gujarat fired 120 Round with in 10 Minute by .177 Calibre Air Rifle  –  Single Shot Breakage. They hit the target (6 * 6 Sports Paper) from Distance 10 Metres, and between each round they changing bullet after every single shoot at the target and reloading it again to fire the next one. Event was organized by Porbandar District Rifle Shooting Association on 26th June, 2014 at Porbandar Rifle Club Shooting Range, M.G.Road, Porbandar, Gujarat, India.
