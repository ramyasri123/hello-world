MAXIMUM NUMBER OF PHOTOS CLICKED BY SINGLE PHOTOGRAPHER

 JAN 19, 2016  

Umesh P. Chawak (Born on 15th December, 1963) of Pune, Maharashtra set new world record by clicking 26555 still photos in 10 hours with boys and girls model on 16th January, 2016 at Pune Maharashtra.

 

BIGGEST POT USING NEWSPAPER WASTE

 NOV 23, 2015  

Ms. Neelu Gunvantbhai Patel (Born on October 1, 1973) Artist and Sculptor of Ahmadabad, Gujarat, designed biggest pot measuring 16 Feet in Height and 6 Feet in Radius Diameter using recycle news paper stick and gum within a 1 month period and displayed at Ahmedabad, Gujarat on 17th November, 2015.

 

LARGEST DOOR HANGING TORAN
Nov 23, 2015
Ms. Dhara Bhoot (Born on November 3, 1989) of Jamkhambhalia, Dist. Devbhumi Dwarka, Gujarat, designed 72 meters and 69 centimeters long door hanging toran using traditional bandhni cloth material and displayed on November 3, 2015.

RED-FORT (MONUMENTS) BY WOODEN ICE-CREAM STICKS

 NOV 17, 2015  

Kandikuppa Chakradhara Rao (Born on jun 15, 1984) Artist of Dasumanthapuram Village of Veeraghattam Mandal in Srikakulam district, Andhrapradesh, designed Red Fort Monument by using a total of 400 wooden ice-cream sticks on August 14th, 2014.
