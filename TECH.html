India’s Youngest Paramotoring Pilot (Licensed)

 JUN 17, 2015  

Ravi Kanabar  (Born on 21st June 1994) of Jamjodhpur, Gujarat  has become India’s youngest Paramotoring pilot at the age of 20 years. He has received Paramotoring Pilot License (License No. – 139) on January 08, 2015 from FAI (Federation  Aeronautique Internationale, Switerzerland ) issued by ACI (Aro Club India ) New Delhi, India.  He has also completed paragliding training from Billing Paragliding Association, Kangra, Himachal Pradesh and became a Paragliding Pilot on 22nd October 2013.

ABDUL RAHIL ABDUL SATTAR SHAIKH (Born on December  28, 1993) of Dhule,  Maharashtra created 2453 Folders on computer desktop using computer mouse with in 1 Hour time period on 6th February 2015 at Dhule, Maharashtra.

Largest Online Library of Indian Literature

 JUL 4, 2013  

Mr. Lalit Kumar founded Kavita Kosh on 05 July 2006 and gradually involved online community of volunteers to develop this library. As on 26 June 2013, Kavita Kosh has 60,229 pages containing poetry. Another part of this library, Gadya Kosh has 9,424 pages of prose literature. Mr. Lalit Kumar is the Founder-Director of this project. Other top volunteers are: Mr. Anil Janvijay, Mr. Dharmendra Kumar Singh, Ms. Pratishtha Sharma, Mr. Ashok Shukla, Mr. Ashish Purohit, Mr. Neeraj Daiya, Ms. Sharda Suman, Mr. Dwijendra Dwij, Mr. Prakash Badal, Ms. Shrddha Jain, Ms. Mani Gupta and Mr. Pradeep Jilwane.
