Invented Weight Operated Gravity Energy Wheel

 JUN 29, 2015  

Shripad Vaidya (Born on 5th May, 1969) of Nagpur, Maharashtra invented first ever gravity energy wheel on 1st January 2015 useful to promotion, research and development of gravity energy. Rotation of gravity energy wheel is achieved by gravity using force of the weights and by continuously controlling the change in center of gravity point so as to suit further development of kinetic energy. Gravitational force is practically utilized for continuous rotation of the wheel.  Electricity or fuel consumption is avoided in this design. The diameter of the wheel is 45 cm.
